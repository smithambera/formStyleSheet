The Iron Yard - Indianapolis
Summer 2016 Demo Day

September 5, 2016 | 4:30 - 6:30 PM

It's that time again - Demo Day is here!

Join us after work to witness the culmination of 12 weeks of intensive training at The Iron Yard. Our first Front End Engineering and fourth Rails Engineering classes are excited to demo their final projects to you, the Indianapolis tech community.

Demo Day will kickoff with a brief overview of The Iron Yard and our Engineering curriculums. Then, students will give first-hand demonstrations of their projects. Attendees will be able to talk with our engineers, engaging them in conversation and asking questions about their projects.

Looking to hire? This is the perfect place to see our graduates in action!
